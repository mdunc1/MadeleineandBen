Dress Code

Please dress in semi-formal attire. Light colours and florals are encouraged for the spring celebration.

We would love for our guests to join us in cocktail attire. For those who prefer, formal wear is also welcome.

Feel free to choose any colour that inspires you — the only request is to avoid wearing white or cream.

And of course, kilts are warmly encouraged for those who wish to embrace the tradition!

Most of all, wear what makes you feel comfortable, confident, and ready to celebrate with us.
