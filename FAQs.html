What is a Cèilidh?

The term "cèilidh"(pronounced kay-lee) originates from the Scottish Gaelic word for a social gathering, though now it is largely used to refer to a style of social dancing. It's like a barn dance or bush dance for the Aussies! No experience or dancing prowess necessary (luckily)- there will be a "caller" who will explain the dance before it begins and then guide everyone through the moves as we go.
